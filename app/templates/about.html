About the Paycheck Protection Program

Home All PPP Loan Data About Contact

The Paycheck Protection Program (PPP) is a loan program aimed at helping U.S. businesses impacted by the Coronavirus crisis. These government-funded loans were provided to companies via the U.S. banking system. They had the potential to be forgiven if they were used toward certain expenses, primarily payroll, to incentivize business owners to retain employees.
